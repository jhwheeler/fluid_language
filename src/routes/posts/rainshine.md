I'm raining.  
Nourishing my body  
with tears of joy  
and peace.

Crystallizing jewel drops of life  
from the expansiveness  
of my breath.

I'm shining.  
Rays of love descend  
from my eyes,  
flowing into my heart,  
warming my body  
from within.
